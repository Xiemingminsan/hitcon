COMPANY PROFILE

The Company's main objective is to provide framed Engineering solutions to civil work projects to be carried out in the country and abroad.

HITCON Engineering PLC is specializing in engineering management consulting, studies, design, contract administration and supervision of road and similar development projects. It is established to offer a wide range of civil, structural, environmental and management services through the complete project life cycle of road (transportation), water and infrastructure development projects.

Hitcon Engineering PLC, over the recent years, evaluate the gap in the market on special services like Asset Management and Impact Assessment, that are related to roads.

The potential of HITCON ENGINEERING PLC is built on its staff, with extensive professional experience gained prior to the formation of HITCON ENGINEERING PLC.

Our Services

Among the services that HITCON Engineering PLC provides in the sector of Road projects, the following are some of them:

  • Pre-feasibility and feasibility studies
  • Project preparation, Appraisal and evaluation
  • Road network studies
  • Environmental Impact Assessment studies
  • Topographic survey
  • Subsurface foundation investigation
  • Soil surveys and pavement condition investigation
  • Hydraulics and Structures Investigations
  • Preparation of Detailed Engineering design, Drawing and Tender documents for Roads and Bridges
  • Supervision of Roads and bridges maintenance/rehabilitation/upgrading and construction works
  • Contract Management
  • Road and Bridge condition surveys and detail analysis to define a deterioration model and forecast future need for investment to enhance Road and Bridge conditions using special tools and software such as HDM4.
